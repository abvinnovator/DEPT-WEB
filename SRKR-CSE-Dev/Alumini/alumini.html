SRKREC ALUMNI ASSOCIATION

About SRKR Alumni Association

SRKR Engineering College (SRKREC) has groomed many young men and women to be the world-cass engineers since its establishment in the year 1980. The renowned alumni of SRKREC are now spread across the world, contributing to the society as Engineers, Teachers, Scientists and Entrepreneurs.

The alumni are making SRKREC proud of its hardwork in shaping these talented brains by winning laurels at national and international levels. We are proud to say that our alumni are at the helm of several organizations globally. Today SRKREC alumni have a significant presence in USA, Europe, Middle East and Asia. SRKREC Alumni Association was registered on 27th June 2007 under the Society Registration Act XXXV of 2001. The association has brought all the earlier Alumni groups together and is the only recognised Alumni Association by the college.

Activities :

  • Conducting alumni meets in different parts of the world.
  • Conducting Soft skill training program for students.
  • Organizing Silver jubilee meets.
  • Sponsoring for poor and Meritorious students.
  • Conducting Guest Lectures for students.
  • Sponsoring for department technical events.

Features

Easy to connect

Alumni networks provide a vast pool of connections from different industries and professions, offering networking opportunities that can lead to career growth and new collaborations.

Career Support

Engaged alumni may share job openings, offer referrals, or help in recruiting talented graduates from their alma mater.

Mentorship and Guidance

Alumni can offer valuable advice and mentorship to current students, helping them navigate their academic and professional paths.

Interaction

Alumni might collaborate with current faculty and students on research projects, contributing their expertise and experience.

Social and Community Engagement

Alumni events foster a sense of community, allowing former students to reconnect and build lasting relationships.

Fundraising and Donations

Satisfied alumni are more likely to contribute to the college through donations and fundraising efforts, helping to improve facilities and educational programs.

About

Sagi Rama Krishnam Raju Engineering College (SRKREC), established in 1980, is a self-financed academic institution of coeducation striving to provide a high quality technical education to engineering aspirants. Being one of the premier and well-established technical institutions of the country, it continues to render service to the nation and the world at large with its alumni holding highly prestigious positions and making substantial contribution.
